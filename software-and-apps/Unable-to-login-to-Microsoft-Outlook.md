# Case Study: Unable to login to Microsoft Outlook

## Summary [Briefly describe what happened (1–2 sentences).]
Phil Lester says that every time he tries to login to Microsoft Outlook, the login goes through but it keeps going back to the login page.

## Context [Customer’s device and environment (if known).]
- Browser: Google Chrome
- Support: Call

## Actions Taken
- Asked Phil if he has tried anything to troubleshoot it
- Asked him to see if he could login to his account in incognito
- He was able to
- Guided him to clear his cache
- Asked him to check and see if he could login

## Result [Describe how the issue was resolved or what outcome was achieved.]
Phil was able to login. It was an issue with corrupted or outdated cache.

## Reflection
Always check the easiest step first to see if the problem is solved quickly.
